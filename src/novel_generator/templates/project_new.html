What happens after this

Creating a project is the handoff from setup into actual work.

  1. 1
    Save the project defaults

    This keeps your premise, story brief, chapter targets, and preferred model together for later runs.

  2. 2
    Queue a full draft run

    By default the run pauses after the story bible and outline so you can stop a weak structure before spending hours on chapters.

  3. 3
    Approve, draft, and review

    Once approved, the worker drafts every chapter, performs editorial QA, and exports the manuscript plus a QA report.
